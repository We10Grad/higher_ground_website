Meet the Owner: Kyle Blum

Higher Ground Stump Grinding is owned and operated by Kyle Blum, a local professional who cares about doing the job right and leaving every yard better than he found it.

KB

A Neighbor You Can Trust With Your Yard

Kyle started Higher Ground Stump Grinding after seeing how many homeowners were left with old, unsafe stumps and no clear, straightforward option to remove them. With a background in hands-on work and a passion for clean, well-kept outdoor spaces, he set out to build a stump grinding service focused on quality, safety, and integrity.

When you work with Higher Ground, you’re working directly with Kyle from start to finish. He’s the person answering your questions, providing your bid, and running the equipment in your yard.

What You Can Expect From Kyle

  • Clear communication before, during, and after your job
  • Honest recommendations about which service level fits your plans and budget
  • Respect for your time, your property, and your neighbors
  • A clean, safe worksite when the job is complete

Whether you’re getting your yard ready for new landscaping, improving curb appeal, or eliminating a tripping hazard, Kyle’s goal is to make stump removal simple and stress-free.
